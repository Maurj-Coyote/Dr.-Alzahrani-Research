Proposal to Establish the Institute of AI, Quantum, Fusion Energy, and Semiconductors (AQFS) at CSUSB

Overview

We propose the establishment of the AI, Quantum, Fusion Energy, and Semiconductors (AQFS) Innovation Lab at CSUSB to advance interdisciplinary research and innovation across four transformative technologies. AQFS will serve as a pioneering research hub, leveraging CSUSB’s strengths in artificial intelligence (AI), high-performance computing (HPC), and digital twin technologies to accelerate scientific discovery without the need for large physical infrastructure.

Vision and Purpose

AQFS will unite faculty expertise and student talent to address challenges in artificial intelligence, quantum computing, sustainable energy through nuclear fusion, and semiconductor innovation. These domains represent critical national priorities in workforce development, scientific leadership, and technological competitiveness. AQFS aligns with CSUSB’s mission to lead in research, innovation, and community engagement.

Primary Goals

  • Conduct cutting-edge research in AI, quantum computing, fusion energy, and semiconductor systems using AI-powered simulations and digital twins.
  • Secure competitive research funding from federal agencies (e.g., NSF, DOE, DoD), foundations, and industry.
  • Mentor undergraduate and graduate students, preparing them for STEM careers in high-demand fields.
  • Provide hands-on opportunities to students through research, internships, training, and exposure to industry-relevant projects.
  • Foster collaboration across CSUSB departments and with national laboratories, industry partners, and other universities.
  • Utilize and expand access to the Advanced Cyberinfrastructure Coordination Ecosystem: Services & Support (ACCESS) for HPC.

Resource Needs

  • Dedicated lab space for research meetings, student teams, and collaboration.
  • Modest funding for research assistants, software licenses, and compute credits.
  • Institutional support for grant writing and research dissemination.

Conclusion

The creation of AQFS will position CSUSB at the forefront of innovation in science and technology. This initiative will elevate the university’s research profile, attract top-tier student talent, and open new pathways for regional and national collaboration.
